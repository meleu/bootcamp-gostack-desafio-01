# My Notes

Some notes about what I've learned in this challenge

- `array.findIndex()` returns `-1` when no element passed the given test.

- `console.count(label)` maintains an internal counter specific to `label` and outputs
to stdout the number of times `console.count()` has been called with the given `label`.

- **calling a middleware in all requests**: just use `server.use(middlewareFunction)`


## Side learnings

- DevDocs.io is an **AWESOME** tool to have on your computer (and in your phone)!

- insomnia is a good app to test REST requests (runs on Linux).

- nodemon offers a good way to restart your project right after saving your code.
